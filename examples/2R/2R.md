Below is the DH-parameter table for a 2R robotic arm with link lengths L1 and L2


| i | $\alpha_{i-1}$ | $a_{i-1}$ | $d_i$ | $\theta_i$ |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 1 | 0 | L1 | 0 | $\theta_1$|
| 2 | 0 | L2 | 0 | $\theta_2$ |

It is as if frame 0 is rotated in anticlockwise manner by an angle $\theta_1$ about its axis $Z_0$ to form a new frame, say A, and then A is translated by length L1 along its axis $X_A$ to finally form frame 1.

Similarly frame 1 is first rotated in anticlockwise manner by an angle $\theta_2$ about its axis $Z_1$ to form a new frame B, which is then translated by length L2 along its X axis to become frame 2.
